### Phase 10 — Comments
- Comment count on VideoItem reflects the count at feed-load time. No real-time update — requires feed refresh to reflect new comments from other users.

### Phase 11 — Profile Page
- `?videoId=` tap-to-navigate falls back silently to top of feed if the video is from a past edition. Cross-edition navigation deferred to a future milestone.
- `VideoGrid` uses `preload="metadata"` for thumbnails — loads a small video segment per cell. If a CDN `poster` URL becomes available, switch to that for instant thumbnails with zero video network cost.

### Total: 4 items across 3 phases — no blockers
